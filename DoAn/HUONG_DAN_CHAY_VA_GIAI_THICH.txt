1. Muc tieu du an
- Day la he thong goi y san pham mua kem cho cua hang cong nghe.
- Frontend hien thi danh sach san pham va goi y AI.
- Backend FastAPI tra du lieu san pham va goi y.
- AI o day la tap luat mua kem duoc rut ra tu du lieu giao dich bang FP-Growth.
- SQLite dong vai tro kho san pham thuc te de frontend hien thi.

2. Luong chay tong the
1. `tao_data.py` sinh du lieu giao dich mau va co chu dong chen loi du lieu.
2. `train_model.py` lam sach du lieu, khai pha luat mua kem, xuat ra:
   - `tap_luat_goi_y.csv`
   - `top_products.csv`
3. `setup_db.py` tao `database.db` va nap danh sach san pham demo.
4. `main.py` nap cac file CSV va mo API.
5. `index.html` goi API de hien thi san pham va nhan goi y mua kem.

3. Cach chay
- Tao moi truong ao:
  `python -m venv .venv`
- Kich hoat:
  Windows: `.venv\Scripts\activate`
- Cai thu vien:
  `pip install fastapi uvicorn pandas numpy mlxtend fuzzywuzzy python-Levenshtein`
- Sinh data:
  `python tao_data.py`
- Train luat:
  `python train_model.py`
- Tao database:
  `python setup_db.py`
- Chay backend:
  `uvicorn main:app --reload`
- Mo `index.html` bang Live Server hoac trinh duyet.

4. Giai thich tung file

4.1. `tao_data.py`
- Nhiem vu: tao bo du lieu giao dich gia lap de mo phong lich su mua hang.
- Danh sach `items` la cac nhom san pham chuan ma he thong quan tam.
- Script tao `10000` hoa don voi 4 kich ban chinh:
  - mua dien thoai thi hay mua kem tai nghe, dong ho
  - mua laptop thi hay mua kem chuot, ban phim
  - mua desktop thi hay mua kem man hinh, chuot, ban phim
  - mua le te thi lay ngau nhien 1-2 mon
- Muc dich cua cac kich ban nay la tao ra mau mua sam de FP-Growth co the hoc duoc quy luat.
- Sau khi sinh du lieu sach, script co tinh “lam ban” du lieu:
  - chen gia tri rong `NaN`
  - viet sai chinh ta nhu `smatphone`, `mause`
  - nhan ban ghi trung lap
- Y nghia: chung minh buoc lam sach du lieu trong `train_model.py` la can thiet.
- Ket qua xuat ra `data_goc.csv`.

4.2. `train_model.py`
- Nhiem vu: bien du lieu thô thanh tri thuc goi y.
- Buoc 1: doc `data_goc.csv`.
- Buoc 2: lam sach du lieu:
  - xoa dong thieu ten san pham
  - xoa dong trung lap
  - sua loi chinh ta bang fuzzy matching
- Ham `tu_dong_sua_ten()`:
  - dua ten loi ve chu thuong
  - so khop voi danh muc chuan
  - neu diem tuong dong >= 80 thi doi thanh ten chuan
- Buoc 3: tinh `top_products.csv`
  - dem tan suat xuat hien cua moi san pham
  - lay top 20 lam co che fallback
- Ly do can fallback:
  - neu san pham khong co luat goi y manh
  - hoac san pham moi, it du lieu
  - thi web van co goi y thay the, tranh man hinh trong
- Buoc 4: tao “gio hang” theo ma hoa don
  - gom cac san pham trong cung `Ma_Hoa_Don` thanh 1 list
  - chi giu nhung gio hang co tu 2 mon tro len
- Ly do bo gio hang 1 mon:
  - khong the rut ra quy luat mua kem neu chi co 1 san pham
- Buoc 5: ma hoa du lieu bang `TransactionEncoder`
  - doi moi gio hang thanh vector True/False
  - dinh dang nay phu hop de chay FP-Growth
- Buoc 6: chay `fpgrowth()`
  - `min_support = 10 / total_baskets` nghia la mot cap phai xuat hien it nhat 10 lan
  - `max_len = 2` nghia la chi tim luat 1 -> 1 cho phu hop giao dien goi y
- Buoc 7: tao association rules
  - dung `confidence` de do do tin cay
  - loc them `lift > 1.2` de giu lai cac luat co gia tri thuc su
- Cuoi cung xuat:
  - `tap_luat_goi_y.csv`: danh sach luat mua kem
  - `top_products.csv`: danh sach san pham pho bien

4.3. `setup_db.py`
- Nhiem vu: tao kho san pham de frontend hien thi san pham thuc.
- Script xoa bang `products` cu neu da ton tai.
- Tao lai bang `products` gom:
  - `id`
  - `category`
  - `name`
  - `price`
  - `icon`
- Sau do nap 64 san pham demo vao SQLite.
- Vai tro cua file nay:
  - AI chi goi y theo category nhu `mouse`, `headphone`
  - frontend can san pham cu the nhu “Tai nghe Sony...” hay “Chuot Logitech...”
  - database giup anh xa tu category sang san pham thuc te

4.4. `main.py`
- Nhiem vu: cung cap API cho frontend.
- `FastAPI()` tao ung dung backend.
- `CORSMiddleware` cho phep frontend va backend khac cong van goi duoc nhau.
- Khi app khoi dong:
  - doc `tap_luat_goi_y.csv`
  - doc `top_products.csv`
  - neu chua co file thi tao DataFrame rong de tranh sap app

- Ham `get_real_product_from_db(category_name)`:
  - mo ket noi `database.db`
  - tim ngau nhien 1 san pham thuoc category duoc goi y
  - dong ket noi ngay sau khi doc
  - neu khong tim thay thi tra ve 1 object placeholder
- Y nghia:
  - tap luat chi cho biet “nen goi y nhom nao”
  - ham nay doi “nhom” thanh “san pham that” de dua len giao dien

- API `/api/products`
  - lay toan bo san pham tu bang `products`
  - frontend dung endpoint nay de do san pham len trang chu

- API `/api/recommend?item=...&limit=4`
  - dau vao la category san pham dang xem
  - xu ly theo 3 pha:
1. Tim cac luat trong `rules_df` ma ve trai chua `item`
2. Sap xep theo `confidence` giam dan
3. Lay `consequents` lam danh sach category goi y
4. Loai trung lap, bo chinh category dang xem, cat theo `limit`
5. Neu khong co luat hop le thi lay `top_products.csv` lam fallback
6. Goi `get_real_product_from_db()` de doi category sang san pham cu the
7. Tra JSON cho frontend

- Truong `source` trong response dung de frontend biet:
  - `association_rules`: goi y den tu luat AI
  - `popular_fallback`: goi y den tu top san pham pho bien

4.5. `index.html`
- Nhiem vu: giao dien nguoi dung.
- Dung TailwindCSS de trang tri nhanh.
- Dung Font Awesome de hien thi icon san pham.
- Giao dien co 3 man:
  - `view-home`: trang chu
  - `view-detail`: chi tiet san pham + goi y AI
  - `view-cart`: gio hang

- Bien toan cuc:
  - `productDB`: cache danh sach san pham lay tu backend
  - `cart`: danh sach mon da them vao gio
  - `currentItem`: san pham dang xem

- Ham `initApp()`
  - goi `http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/products`
  - luu ket qua vao `productDB`
  - sau do moi hien trang chu
  - neu backend chua chay thi hien thong bao loi

- Ham `navigateTo(viewName, productId)`
  - an tat ca cac view
  - hien view duoc chon
  - neu vao chi tiet thi goi `renderDetail(productId)`
  - neu vao gio hang thi goi `renderCart()`

- Ham `renderHome()`
  - lay ngau nhien 10 san pham tu `productDB`
  - do len grid trang chu

- Ham `renderDetail(productId)`
  - tim san pham dang duoc chon
  - do thong tin len giao dien
  - goi API `/api/recommend`
  - neu co goi y thi do ra cac the san pham mua kem
  - dong thoi dua nhan de phan biet goi y AI hay top trending

- Ham `addToCart(product)`
  - them san pham vao mang `cart`
  - cap nhat so luong tren icon gio hang
  - doi mau nut tam thoi de bao them thanh cong

- Ham `renderCart()`
  - hien tung san pham trong gio
  - tinh tong tien
  - hien thong diep neu gio hang rong

- Ham `removeFromCart(index)`
  - xoa san pham khoi gio
  - cap nhat lai tong so luong va tong tien

5. Y nghia cac file du lieu
- `data_goc.csv`: du lieu giao dich tho, chua loi va trung lap.
- `tap_luat_goi_y.csv`: ket qua hoc luat mua kem.
- `top_products.csv`: top san pham pho bien de fallback.
- `database.db`: kho san pham de frontend hien thi.

6. Diem can nho khi thuyet trinh
- Day khong phai mo hinh deep learning, ma la he goi y dua tren Association Rules.
- AI nam o buoc khai pha luat mua kem tu lich su giao dich.
- Frontend khong tu tinh goi y, ma chi goi API backend.
- Backend tach 2 vai tro:
  - doc tap luat AI
  - doc SQLite de tra san pham thuc te
- Fallback la diem quan trong vi giai quyet truong hop khong co luat.
- Buoc lam sach du lieu cung quan trong vi du lieu ban dau co chu dong gan loi.

7. Tom tat ngan gon
- `tao_data.py`: tao du lieu mau.
- `train_model.py`: lam sach va hoc luat.
- `setup_db.py`: tao kho san pham.
- `main.py`: mo API.
- `index.html`: hien thi va goi backend.
